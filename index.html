Welcome

Welcome to the Biomaterials and Regenerative Engineering Alliance Laboratory (B-REAL) at Westlake University. Our research interests are to investigate a methodology for functional biomaterial development and use biomaterials as a tool to solve unmet clinical problems.

Positions Available

We are now hiring!

Position: Postdoctoral Fellows (4-5)
Requirements:

1) Candidates should have a background in any of the following discipline: chemistry, physics, materials, biology, medicine, biomedical engineering, chemical engineering, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering.

2) Candidates should have research experience in any of the following fields: (a) biomaterials; (b) tissue engineering and regenerative medicine; (c) organic luminescent materials, optoelectrical materials, dielectric materials, photoacoustic materials and device micro/nano-fabrication; (d) medical imaging and applications; (e) stem cell manufacturing and stem cell differentiation; (f) gene editing; (g) immunotherapy; (h) implant biocompatibility; (i) biomanufacturing and 3D printing; (j) Simulation and theoretical calculation (FEM, DFT or molecular dynamics); (k) artificial intelligence and machine learning in materials, chemistry, and biology research; (l) surgeries and animal models.

3) Candidates should have a PhD degree or will obtain a Ph.D. degree before joining as a postdoc.

4) Candidates should have clear career goals, be ambitious, hard-working, and good at communications and cooperation.

5) Candidates should be effective in English communicating and writing and have published at least one paper in top journals.

Compensation and Benefits

The research team offers a competitive compensation package commensurate with the candidate’s qualifications and experience.
Postdoc(s) selected for China Postdoctoral Science Foundation or Zhejiang Province Selected Funding for Postdoctoral Research Projects will be entitled to further receive a supporting fund of the same amount from the Hangzhou municipal government.
Postdoc(s) who work in Hangzhou on a full-time basis after completing their postdoctoral research will be eligible for applying an allowance of 400,000RMB from the Hangzhou municipal government.

How to Apply

To apply, please send the following documents in English in one PDF format to yangjian07@westlake.edu.cn, and indicate “Postdoc Application + Candidate Name” in the email subject.
CV
2 reference letters (at least one letter from the candidate’s PhD mentor or postdoc advisor)
Doctoral Degree Certificate
